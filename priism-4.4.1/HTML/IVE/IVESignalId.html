Reference: IVESignalId

Overview

In C, an IVESignalId is used as a handle to an event source and the associated callback which were registered with IVEAddSignal. This handle may be passed to IVENoticeSignal or IVERemoveSignal,

Include ive_eventloop.h to declare IVESignalId.
