Reference: IVERemoveAllEventTriggers

Overview

Removes any callbacks installed with IVEAddInput, IVEAddSignal, IVEAddTimer, IVEAddWorkProc, or IVESetMessageHandler for one event context. The IDs associated with any of the canceled callbacks must not be used in subsequent calls to IVERemoveInput, IVERemoveSignal, IVERemoveTimer, or IVERemoveWorkProc.

Prototype (C)

#include "ive_eventloop.h"
void IVERemoveAllEventTriggers(IVEEventContext context);
