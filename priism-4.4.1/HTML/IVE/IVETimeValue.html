Reference: IVETimeValue

Overview

In C, an IVETimeValue an absolute time measurement. It should be treated as an opaque type: the only operations that are defined for an IVETimeValue are IVEGetCurrTime and IVECalcTimeDiff.

Include ive_standards.h and ive_time.h to declare IVETimeValue. Beware that inclusion of ive_standards.h sets feature-set macros. It is safest to include it before any other header.
