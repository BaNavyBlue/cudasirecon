Reference: IVEShmDestroyHold

Overview

Releases any resources associated with a hold initialized by IVEShmInitHold.

Prototype (C)

#include "ive_standards.h"
#include "ive_shm.h"
void IVEShmDestroyHold(IVEShmHold* p_hold);

Prototype (Fortran)

There is no Fortran interface.

Parameters

p_hold
p_hold points to the hold to be destroyed.

Side effects

Inclusion of ive_standards.h sets certain feature-set macros. It is safest to include it before any other headers to avoid inconsistent definitions.

Cross references

IVEShmInitHold, IVEShmAcquireHold, IVEShmTryAcquireHold, IVEShmReleaseHold, and IVEShmHold
