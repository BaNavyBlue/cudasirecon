Reference: IVEShmRWLock

Overview

An IVEShmRWLock represents a read-write lock which may be used to synchronize access to a resource shared by multiple threads in one or more processes. A read-write lock provides two levels of access to the resource: non-exclusive (i.e. read-only) access and exclusive (read/write) access. An IVEShmRWLock may provide better performance than an IVEShmMutex in cases where a shared resource is more frequently read than modified.

Include ive_standards.h and ive_shm.h to declare IVEShmRWLock. Beware that inclusion of ive_standards.h sets feature-set macros. It is safest to include it before any other header.
