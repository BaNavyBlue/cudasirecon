Reference: IVEPlugin

Overview

An IVEPlugin is used to represent a chunk of dynamically loaded code. Load a plugin with IVELoadPlugin, get the address of a plugin's attributes with IVEGetPluginAttr, and unload a plugin with IVEUnloadPlugin.
