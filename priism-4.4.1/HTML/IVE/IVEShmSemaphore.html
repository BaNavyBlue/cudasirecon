Reference: IVEShmSemaphore

Overview

An IVEShmSemaphore is a simple synchronization device representing an integer that supports two important operations: atomically incrementing the integer's value (IVEIncrementSemaphore) and atomically testing the integer's value against zero and decrementing it if it is greater than zero (IVEShmDecrementSemaphore or IVEShmTryDecrementSemaphore).

Include ive_standards.h and ive_shm.h to declare IVEShmSemaphore. Beware that inclusion of ive_standards.h sets feature-set macros. It is safest to include it before any other header.
