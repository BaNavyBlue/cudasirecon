Reference: IVEThreadDestroyMutex

Overview

Releases any resources associated with a mutual exclusion lock initialized by IVEThreadInitMutex.

Prototype (C)

#include "ive_standards.h"
#include "ive_thread.h"
void IVEThreadDestroyMutex(IVEThreadMutex* p_mutex);

Prototype (Fortran)

There is no Fortran interface.

Parameters

p_mutex
p_mutex points to the mutex to be destroyed.

Side effects

Inclusion of ive_standards.h sets certain feature-set macros. It is safest to include it before any other headers to avoid inconsistent definitions.
