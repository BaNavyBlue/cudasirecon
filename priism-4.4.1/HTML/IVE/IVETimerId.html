Reference: IVETimerId

Overview

In C, an IVETimerId is used as a handle to an event source and the associated callback which were registered with IVEAddTimer. This handle may be passed to IVERemoveTimer to cancel the timer before it expires.

Include ive_eventloop.h to declare IVETimerId.
