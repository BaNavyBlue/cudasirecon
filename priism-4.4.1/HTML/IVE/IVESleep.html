Reference: IVESleep

Overview

IVESleep suspends execution of the caller until at least a specified number of microseconds has elapsed.

Prototype (C)

#include "ive_time.h"
void IVESleep(IVETimeInterval intvl_us);

Prototype (Fortran)

subroutine IVESleep(intvl_us)
integer intvl_us

Parameters

intvl_us
intvl_us is the number of microseconds for which execution should be suspended.
