Reference: IVEThreadOnceFlag

Overview

A variable of the type IVEThreadOnceFlag is used with IVEThreadCallOnce to ensure that an operation is performed once. Only two operations on an IVEThreadOnceFlag are defined: static initialization with the constant IVE_ONCE_INIT and use as an argument to IVEThreadCallOnce.

Include ive_standards.h and ive_thread.h to declare IVEThreadOnceFlag and IVE_ONCE_INIT. Beware that inclusion of ive_standards.h sets feature-set macros. It is safest to include it before any other header.
