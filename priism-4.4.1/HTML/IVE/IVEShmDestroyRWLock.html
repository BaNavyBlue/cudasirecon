Reference: IVEShmDestroyRWLock

Overview

Releases any resources associated with a read-write lock initialized by IVEShmInitRWLock.

Prototype (C)

#include "ive_standards.h"
#include "ive_shm.h"
void IVEShmDestroyRWLock(IVEShmRWLock* p_lock);

Prototype (Fortran)

There is no Fortran interface.

Parameters

p_lock
p_lock points to the lock to be destroyed.

Side effects

Inclusion of ive_standards.h sets certain feature-set macros. It is safest to include it before any other headers to avoid inconsistent definitions.
