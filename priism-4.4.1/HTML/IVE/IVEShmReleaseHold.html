Reference: IVEShmReleaseHold

Overview

Unlocks a hold.

Prototype (C)

#include "ive_standards.h"
#include "ive_shm.h"
void IVEShmReleaseHold(IVEShmHold* p_hold);

Prototype (Fortran)

There is no Fortran interface.

Parameters

p_hold
p_hold points to the hold to be unlocked. Only the thread which locked the hold should unlock it.

Side effects

Inclusion of ive_standards.h sets certain feature-set macros. It is safest to include it before any other headers to avoid inconsistent definitions.

Cross references

IVEShmInitHold, IVEShmDestroyHold, IVEShmAcquireHold, IVEShmTryAcquireHold, and IVEShmHold
