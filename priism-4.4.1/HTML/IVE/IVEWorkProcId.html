Reference: IVEWorkProcId

Overview

In C, an IVEWorkProcId is used as a handle to a work procedure registered with IVEAddWorkProc. This handle may be passed to IVERemoveWorkProc to cancel the work procedure.

Include ive_eventloop.h to declare IVEWorkProcId.
