Reference: IVEInputId

Overview

In C, an IVEInputId is used as a handle to an event source and the associated callback which were registered with IVEAddInput. This handle may be passed to IVERemoveInput to stop the flow of events from the source.

Include ive_eventloop.h to declare IVEInputId.
