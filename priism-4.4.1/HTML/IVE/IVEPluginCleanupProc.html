Reference: IVEPluginCleanupProc

Overview

IVEPluginCleanupProc is the prototype for the routine called by IVEUnloadPlugin to free resources allocated during plugin initialization. As shown below, the routine is passed one argument, the address of the plugin's attributes.

Include ive_plugin.h to declare IVEPluginInitProc.

Prototype (C)

void (*IVEPluginCleanupProc)(void* attr);

Parameters

attr
Is the address, set by the plugin's initialization function, of the plugin's attributes.
